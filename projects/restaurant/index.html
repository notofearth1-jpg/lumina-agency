Our Story

A Legacy of
Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2010, Spice Garden began as a small family kitchen with a big dream — to bring the authentic flavors of India to every plate. Today, we're proud to be Ahmedabad's favorite destination for traditional cuisine with a modern twist.

Our chefs blend age-old recipes with contemporary techniques, creating dishes that honor tradition while delighting the modern palate.
