THE AURA METHOD
Our Process
I
Concept

We begin by understanding the essence of the space and the people who inhabit it. Sketching the unseen.

II
Design

Translating emotion into geometry. Every line, texture, and light source is meticulously planned.

III
Build

The realization of the vision. We oversee craftsmanship to ensure the highest fidelity to the design.

IV
Curate

The final layer. Selecting art, flora, and objects that bring the space to life.
